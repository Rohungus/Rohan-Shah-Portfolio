#3
Student Documentary
AI: Our Future or Our End?

This was a full end-to-end production that I organized.

I used this opportunity to make a documentary about something that I and the people around me were curious about. AI was constantly being thrown around, and most people didn't even know what it was.

It was a fun challenge to try and make an esoteric concept more digestible for a wider audience.

Ultimately, this doc was awarded Best Visual Arts at CSU's Tower Day and presented at the Georgia Collegiate Honors Conference.

Penguin's Detour

This project, Penguin's Detour is a short story I wrote about penguins, polar bears, and gentrification.

I heard a song called Penguin's Detour and imagined a whole world that would approach a complex topic through the lens of adorable penguins that ran a cab company.

Eventually this story was selected as a finalist and published in the Agnes Scott College Writer’s Festival.
